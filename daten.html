GLOBAL FOOTPRINT NETWORK

Our time-series graphs map out the gap between human demand on nature (Ecological Footprint)
and nature’s capacity to meet that demand (biological capacity) for over 200 countries and regions from 1961.
A country is running an ecological deficit if its Ecological Footprint exceeds its biocapacity.
It has an ecological reserve if its biocapacity exceeds its Footprint.
